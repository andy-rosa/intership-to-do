Todo-List App

Welcome to the Todo-List App, your ultimate tool for staying organized and managing your tasks efficiently. Whether you're a student, professional, or anyone looking to keep track of their daily responsibilities, our app is designed to help you stay productive and focused.

Features

  1. Task Management

    Our app provides a user-friendly interface for managing your tasks effectively. You can easily add new tasks, edit existing ones, and mark them as completed when you're done. With a clear overview of your tasks, you'll never miss a deadline again.

  2. Task Prioritization

    Prioritizing tasks is crucial for efficient time management. Our app allows you to assign priorities to your tasks, ensuring that you focus on what's most important. You can categorize tasks as high, medium, or low priority, helping you stay organized and tackle the most critical tasks first.
